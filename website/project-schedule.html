General Schedule

Order of Deliverables and Goals
  • Step 1: Obtain project information
  • Step 2: Meet with project mentor and USGS in order to get a more detailed project description
  • Step 3: Research tools, databases, and programs relevant to our project. This includes the already implemented solution in OpenLayers as well as Leaflet and other JavaScript packages we may be using.
  • Step 4: Begin technical feasability and design phase
  • Step 5: Draft technical feasability document for mentor review
  • Step 6: Complete technical feasability document and review with USGS
  • Step 7: Begin requirements specification and protype design
  • Step 8: Review requirements specification and prototype design with mentor and client
  • Step 9: Begin technical prototype phase
  • Step 10: Demo prototype for mentor and USGS team
  • Step 11: Deliver Minimum Viable Product and begin testing
  • Step 12: Begin implementation of stretch goals and continue community testing
  • Step 13: Demo stretch goal prototype and review testing results
  • Step 14: Lock in final deliverable product goals, fix bugs, and implement community suggestions
  • Step 15: Implement final deliverable product
  • Step 16: Deliver final product to USGS team and present to Capstone Conference
